# Tiny Habits

### Question 1: In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?
* The most interesting idea was that we don’t fail because we lack motivation, but because we make habits too big. BJ Fogg explained that starting very small (tiny habits) makes change easier and more successful.

---

### Question 2: How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P.
* B = Behavior happens when Motivation, Ability, and Prompt come together.
* M = Motivation (your desire to do something)
* A = Ability (how easy it is to do)
* P = Prompt (the trigger that reminds you)
* To make habits easier, we should increase ability by making the habit very small and simple.

---

### Question 3: Why is it important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of habit?
* Celebration creates positive emotions in the brain.
* Positive emotions help the brain remember and repeat the behavior.
* Whatever we celebrate becomes a habit because our brain connects it with feeling good.

### Question 5: What is the book's perspective about Identity?
* The book says real change happens when we change our identity.
* Instead of saying “I want to read,” we should say “I am a reader.”
* Habits shape our identity, and identity shapes our habits.

### Question 6: Write about the book's perspective on how to make a habit easier to do?
* Make the cue obvious.
* Make the habit attractive.
* Make it easy and small.
* Reduce friction.
* Prepare the environment in advance.

---

### Question 7: Write about the book's perspective on how to make a habit harder to do?
* Make the cue invisible.
* Make it unattractive.
* Increase difficulty.
* Add friction.
* Remove triggers from your environment.

## Reflection

### Question 8:
Pick one habit that you would like to do more of? What are the steps that you can take to make the cue obvious or the habit more attractive or easy and/or response satisfying?

* Habit: Daily coding practice.
* I will keep my laptop ready on my desk.
* I will start with just 20 minutes.
* I will fix a specific time every day.
* I will track my progress.
* I will celebrate after completing the session.

---

### Question 9:
Pick one habit that you would like to eliminate or do less of? What are the steps that you can take to make the cue invisible or the process unattractive or hard or the response unsatisfying?

* Habit: Using social media too much.
* I will remove social media apps from my phone.
* I will turn off notifications.
* I will block websites during study hours.
* I will keep my phone away while studying.
