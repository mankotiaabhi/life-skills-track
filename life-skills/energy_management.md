# ENERGY MANAGEMENT

## Question 1:
### What activities help you relax (Calm quadrant)?

- Doing nothing and taking quiet time  
- Going for a walk  
- Working out  

---

## Question 2:
### When do you find yourself entering the Stress quadrant?

- When I am unable to complete a task  
- When I start overthinking  

---

## Question 3:
### How do you know you are in the Excitement quadrant?

- I feel energetic  
- I feel happy  
- I feel motivated to complete the task at hand  

---

## Question 4:
### Paraphrase of "Sleep is Your Superpower" (Points Only)

- Get a full night of sleep, no matter what  
- Sleep is a non-negotiable necessity  
- Sleep deprivation negatively affects physical and mental health  
- Use the bed only for sleeping  
- Keep the room cool for better sleep quality  
- Maintain a consistent sleep schedule  

---

## Question 5:
### Ideas to Sleep Better

- Sleep in a cool room (using a blanket if needed)  
- Get sunlight exposure in the morning to regulate your sleep cycle  
- Avoid using or charging your phone before bed  
- Do light exercise or a short walk in the evening  
- Sleep at the same time every day  
- Use the bed only for sleeping, not for other activities  

---

## Question 6:
### Paraphrase of "Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise" (Points Only)

- Improves long-term memory by generating new brain cells in the hippocampus  
- Increases blood flow to the brain  
- Improves sleep quality  
- Enhances focus and learning retention  
- Elevates mood and balances emotions  
- Delays age-related brain diseases  
- Increases overall brain volume  

---

## Question 7:
### Steps to Exercise More

- Practice shadow boxing regularly  
- Do push-ups and squats after waking up  
- Move every 30 minutes if sitting for long periods  
- Prioritize proper sleep and recovery  
- Take a short walk after meals
