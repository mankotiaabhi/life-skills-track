# Focus Management

## Question 1  
**What is Deep Work?**  

Deep Work means working with full focus on one important task without any distractions. It helps you do your best work.

## Question 3  
**How can you use these ideas in daily life?**

- Set clear goals with deadlines  
- Plan fixed time for focused work  
- Keep separate time for breaks and distractions  
- Avoid multitasking  
- Stay disciplined and consistent

## Question 4  
**What are the dangers of social media?**

- Wastes productive time  
- Reduces focus and attention  
- Can affect sleep  
- Spreads misinformation  
- Can cause stress or depression  
- May influence negative or extreme thinking
